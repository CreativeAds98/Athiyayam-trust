A Journey of Compassion

Athiyayam Trust was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful belief: No one should be deprived of basic human rights due to poverty. What started as a small initiative by a group of like-minded volunteers to feed the homeless during the pandemic has now grown into a fully registered charitable trust.

Our journey is fueled by the smiles of the children we educate, the relief of the patients we treat, and the dignity of the women we empower. We believe in transparency, direct impact, and sustainable change.

Today, we work across 15+ districts, touching thousands of lives annually through our focused programs in education, healthcare, and disaster relief.
